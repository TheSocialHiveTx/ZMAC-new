Home Repair

Roof
Repair.

From a few missing shingles after a windstorm to a persistent leak that's been causing interior damage, ZMAC Construction handles residential roof repairs of all sizes — quickly, correctly, and with materials that match your existing roof system.

Most residential roof problems fall into a few common categories: storm damage (missing or damaged shingles from wind or hail), flashing failures (at chimneys, skylights, walls, or valleys), age-related deterioration (cracked or curling shingles, granule loss), and installation defects (improper flashing, inadequate ventilation, or poor workmanship from a previous contractor). ZMAC's technicians are experienced with all of these failure modes and can diagnose the root cause quickly — not just treat the visible symptom.

One of the most important services we provide is an honest assessment of whether repair or replacement is the right choice for your situation. If your roof is less than 15 years old and the damage is isolated, repair is almost always the right answer. If your roof is approaching or past its expected lifespan and showing widespread deterioration, we'll tell you that too — because a repair on a failing roof is often money wasted. We give you the information you need to make the right decision for your home and budget.

The Repair Methodology.

1

Source Mapping

Using diagnostic tools to find the exact origin of a leak, which is often far from the visible stain.

2

Component Stripping

Removing affected shingles or flashing to expose the underlying deck or structural components.

3

Reconstruction

Installing premium ice & water shield and matched materials to restore system integrity.

4

Integrity Test

Final water-testing of the repaired area to ensure the leak is permanently resolved.

Frequently Asked Questions.

We handle leak detection and repair, shingle replacement, flashing repair, gutter service, storm damage, and general wear-and-tear repairs.

Non-emergency repairs are typically scheduled within 48 hours. For active leaks and emergencies, we offer same-day service.

Repair is cheaper in the short term, but if your roof is over 15-20 years old or has widespread damage, replacement is often a better long-term investment. We provide honest assessments based on your roof's condition.

Most insurance policies cover repairs for damage caused by sudden events like hail or wind. Wear-and-tear or maintenance issues are typically not covered. We can help you document damage for a potential claim.

Look for water stains on ceilings, damp spots in the attic, peeling paint near rooflines, or missing/curled shingles. If you see any of these signs, schedule a repair inspection immediately.
