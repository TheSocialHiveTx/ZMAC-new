Preventive Care

Roof
Maintenance.

Protect your roofing investment with ZMAC Construction's commercial roof maintenance programs. Regular, proactive maintenance is the single most cost-effective thing you can do to extend your roof's life, prevent costly emergency repairs, and maintain your manufacturer warranty.

Most commercial roofing warranties require documented semi-annual or annual inspections to remain valid. Beyond warranty compliance, regular maintenance catches small problems — a lifted seam, a cracked flashing, a clogged drain — before they become major leaks that damage your building interior and disrupt your operations. Studies consistently show that every dollar spent on preventive roof maintenance saves $5–$10 in emergency repair and premature replacement costs.

ZMAC's commercial maintenance programs are customized to your specific roof system, age, and condition. We offer semi-annual inspection programs, annual inspection programs, and comprehensive maintenance agreements that include minor repairs as part of the service. After every inspection, you receive a detailed written report with photos documenting the roof's condition, any issues found, and recommended actions — giving you a clear picture of your roof's health and a documented maintenance record.

FAQ.

A standard commercial roof maintenance visit includes: clearing all drains, scuppers, and gutters of debris; inspecting all seams, laps, and flashings for lifting or cracking; inspecting all penetrations (HVAC units, pipes, skylights) for seal integrity; checking membrane surface condition; identifying any ponding water issues; and performing minor repairs on the spot when possible. You receive a written report with photos after every visit documenting the roof's condition and any work performed.

A standard commercial roof maintenance inspection typically takes 2–4 hours for buildings up to 20,000 square feet, depending on roof complexity and the number of penetrations. We schedule maintenance visits at your convenience and work around your business operations. For buildings with multiple roofs or complex systems, we can schedule multi-day maintenance programs.
