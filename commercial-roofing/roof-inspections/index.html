Inspections.

Comprehensive commercial roof inspections with detailed reports. Identify problems early, prevent costly emergency repairs.

A professional commercial roof inspection is the foundation of smart roof asset management. ZMAC Construction's certified inspectors provide thorough, documented inspections that give you a clear picture of your roof's condition — and the information you need to make smart decisions about maintenance, repair, or replacement.

Commercial roof inspections serve multiple critical purposes. They're required by most manufacturer warranties to keep coverage valid. They're essential after major weather events to document storm damage for insurance claims. They're the starting point for any maintenance or restoration program. And they're a critical part of due diligence when buying or selling a commercial property. ZMAC's inspectors are trained to identify issues that untrained eyes miss — subtle membrane deterioration, early-stage seam failures, inadequate drainage, and flashing conditions that will become leaks within months.

Every ZMAC inspection includes a comprehensive written report with photos documenting the roof's condition, all deficiencies found, and prioritized recommendations. We can also perform infrared moisture scanning to identify wet insulation that isn't visible to the naked eye — a critical tool for understanding the true extent of any water infiltration.

FAQ.

A standard ZMAC commercial roof inspection covers: membrane surface condition (checking for blistering, cracking, granule loss, or UV degradation); all seams and laps (checking for lifting, separation, or inadequate adhesion); all flashings (checking for cracking, lifting, or improper installation); all penetrations including HVAC units, pipes, and skylights (checking for seal integrity); drainage systems including drains, scuppers, and gutters (checking for blockages and proper slope); and edge metal and fascia (checking for corrosion, lifting, or gaps). You receive a written report with photos after every inspection.

A standard commercial roof inspection typically takes 2–4 hours for buildings up to 20,000 square feet. Larger buildings, buildings with multiple roof sections, or inspections that include infrared moisture scanning may take longer. We schedule inspections at your convenience and provide the written report within 2–3 business days of the inspection.
