Roofing
Systems.

ZMAC Construction installs and maintains a full range of commercial and residential roofing systems across the Denver metro area. Choosing the right roofing system for your property is one of the most important decisions you'll make as a property owner — and it requires expertise in both the available materials and the specific demands of Colorado's climate.

Colorado's Front Range presents a uniquely challenging roofing environment. High-altitude UV radiation degrades roofing membranes faster than at lower elevations. Dramatic temperature swings — from sub-zero winter nights to 90-degree summer afternoons — stress seams, flashings, and substrates. Hailstorms that rank among the most severe in the nation can puncture or crack membranes, shingles, and metal panels. And heavy snow loads require roofing systems with adequate structural support and drainage design. ZMAC's team has deep expertise in specifying and installing systems that perform in these conditions.

Whether you need a highly reflective TPO membrane for a flat commercial roof, a durable EPDM system for a low-slope application, a long-lasting metal roof for a residential or commercial property, or an impact-resistant shingle system designed to survive Colorado's hail seasons — ZMAC has the expertise, certifications, and manufacturer relationships to deliver the right system for your project, installed correctly and backed by comprehensive warranties.

Our Systems

What We Install.

Flat Roofing

Expert installation and repair of flat and low-slope roofing systems for commercial properties — including proper drainage design, membrane selection, and flashing details that prevent the leaks common on flat roofs.

TPO Roofing

Thermoplastic Polyolefin (TPO) membranes offer excellent UV reflectivity, heat-weldable seams, and strong puncture resistance — making them one of the most popular and cost-effective commercial flat roofing systems.

EPDM Roofing

Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer (EPDM) rubber roofing is renowned for its exceptional durability, flexibility in extreme temperatures, and proven 30+ year performance record on commercial and low-slope applications.

Metal Roofing

Standing seam and metal panel roofing systems offer 40–70 year lifespans, superior hail and wind resistance, and excellent snow-shedding performance — making them ideal for Colorado's demanding climate.

Sloped Roofing

Sloped roofing systems — including asphalt shingles, architectural shingles, and steep-slope metal — provide effective water shedding, attic ventilation, and curb appeal for residential and commercial properties.

Impact-Resistant Roofing

Class 4 impact-resistant shingles and metal systems are engineered to withstand Colorado's severe hailstorms — and may qualify you for significant insurance premium discounts while providing superior long-term protection.
