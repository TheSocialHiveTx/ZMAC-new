Storm Recovery

Hail
Damage.

Expert hail damage assessment, insurance claims support, and complete roof restoration in Lakewood and across the Denver metro area.

Colorado is one of the most hail-prone states in the country, and the Denver metro area sits squarely in "Hail Alley" — the region that sees more large hail events than almost anywhere else in the US. ZMAC Construction has helped hundreds of property owners navigate hail damage claims and get their roofs restored to full protection.

Hail damage to roofing isn't always obvious from the ground. While large hail (1.5" and above) often causes visible bruising or cracking on shingles, smaller hail can cause significant granule loss that accelerates aging and reduces the roof's ability to shed water — damage that only becomes apparent during a professional inspection. ZMAC's inspectors are trained to identify all forms of hail impact, document the damage thoroughly, and provide the detailed reports that insurance companies require to process claims.

We work directly with all major insurance carriers and can assist you through every step of the claims process — from the initial inspection and damage documentation through the adjuster meeting, claim approval, and final installation. Our goal is to make the process as smooth as possible so you can focus on your business or home while we handle the roofing side.

FAQ.

Hail damage assessment involves a systematic inspection of the entire roof surface, all flashings, and all penetrations. ZMAC's inspectors document every impact point with photos, measure hail impact density across different roof sections, and prepare a detailed written report that meets insurance carrier requirements. For commercial roofs, we also assess whether the membrane integrity has been compromised and whether the damage qualifies for full replacement under your policy. We provide this inspection at no cost when you're filing an insurance claim.

Timeline depends on the scope of damage and the insurance claims process. The initial inspection typically takes 1–3 hours. Insurance claim processing varies by carrier but typically takes 1–4 weeks. Once the claim is approved, ZMAC can typically schedule installation within 1–2 weeks. We'll keep you informed throughout the process and work to complete the project as quickly as possible.

Yes, ZMAC Construction provides free hail damage inspections for both commercial and residential properties. If you've experienced a recent hail event, call 720-355-3780 or submit a request online to schedule your inspection. We'll assess the damage, advise you on whether an insurance claim is warranted, and help you navigate the process from start to finish.

While Colorado statutes vary, most insurance policies require you to file a claim within 1 to 2 years of the date of the storm. However, it's critical to act fast. Hail damage compromises your roof's integrity immediately, and waiting too long can lead to leaks, mold, and secondary damage that may not be covered if you delayed the initial claim.

If you have a Replacement Cost Value (RCV) policy, insurance typically covers the full cost of replacing your hail-damaged roof, minus your deductible. This includes the cost of materials, labor, and even local building code upgrades. We specialize in using Xactimate—the same software adjusters use—to ensure every necessary line item is accounted for.

Pocketing the insurance money without performing the repairs is high-risk. Insurance carriers document every claim; if you don't repair the roof and a future storm causes more damage, they may deny the new claim entirely. Additionally, unrepaired hail damage will be flagged during a home inspection, significantly lowering your property value during a sale.
